Upcoming Events & Ranger Programs

Congaree National Park hosts numerous events throughout the year, including: Firefly Festival (May-June) – Experience the rare and breathtaking sight of synchronous fireflies lighting up the forest at night. Guided Canoe Tours – Join a park ranger for an educational paddle along Cedar Creek. Junior Ranger Program – A fun and interactive way for kids to learn about the park and earn a badge. Starry Nights at Congaree – Stargazing events held in the park’s Dark Sky-designated areas.

Conservation & Preservation Efforts:

The park is committed to preserving its unique ecosystem through: Habitat Restoration – Protecting and maintaining the park’s old-growth trees and wetlands. Wildlife Monitoring – Tracking populations of key species to ensure ecological balance. Community Engagement – Partnering with local organizations to promote conservation
